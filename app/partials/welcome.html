Welcome to Pikr

Making hard decisions easier.

Pikr is a service that helps you make choices with the help of your family, friends, and social circle. Pikr not only helps you make informed choices, it also lets you analyze your friends choices, and gain important insights.

For example, if you are trying to choose new glasses, here's how Pikr can help you. First, pick out the frames you like. Next upload them to Pikr. Once you are ready, email your friends and ask them to rank the pictures. They can also leave a comment with additional information. Pikr collects the data from all participants, and lets you analyze it.
